What is Olin Climate Justice?
Olin Climate Justice (OCJ) is a student activist group advocating for institutional change to combat the climate crisis. We prioritize systemic change and institutional accountability over individual action, and engage in constructive efforts to move Olin towards a just and sustainable future.
